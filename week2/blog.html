Stress Less Smooties

Ingredients:- 1 1/2 c. plain full-fat kefir, chilled 1/2 c. raspberries 1 tbsp. hemp seeds 1 peach, pitted and sliced 1/2 ice

Method:- In blender, puree kefir, raspberries, hemp seeds, peach, and ice until smooth, about 30 sec.

Cotton Candy Milkshake

Ingredients:- Store-bought vanilla frosting, for serving Blue raspberry-flavored popping candy, for serving Red cherry-flavored popping candy, for serving 1 cup blue cotton candy, plus more for garnish 2 pints vanilla ice cream 1/2 cup whole milk

Method:- 1)In a blender, combine the blue cotton candy, 1 pint of the ice cream, 1/4 cup of the milk and 1/2 teaspoon of the vanilla. Blend until smooth. 2)Transfer to a pitcher and rinse out the blender. 3)Top each with some blue and pink cotton candy, a swirl lollipop and a rock candy lollipop. 4)Add straws and drink immediately.

Mojito Pitcher

Ingredients:- ½ cup loosely packed mint leaves plus more for the garnish, ½ cup water, ½ cup sugar, 1 cup lime juice (about 8 limes), plus 1 lime for the garnish, 2 cups white rum, 2 cups soda water or club soda

Method:- 1)Add the sugar, water and mint to a small saucepan and heat over medium heat. 2)Bring to a simmer and stir until all sugar is dissolved. 3)Add the lime juice and rum to the pitcher 4) Add the extra mint leaves and the extra lime, cut into rounds. 5) Fill the pitcher with 4 handfuls of ice 6) Gently stir in soda water just before serving
