Coconut Mojito

Ingredients

  • 6 mint leaves, plus more for garnish
  • 1 ounce white rum
  • 1 ounce coconut rum
  • 1 ounce lime juice
  • 1 ounce cream of coconut (not coconut cream)
  • 2 ounces soda water
Method
  1. In a cocktail shaker, muddle the mint leaves.
  2. Add the white rum, coconut rum, lime juice, and cream of coconut to the shaker and fill the cocktail shaker with ice.
  3. Shake until cold.
  4. Place ice into a glass, and strain in the drink.
  5. Top off the glass with soda water.
  6. Garnish with additional mint leaves.
