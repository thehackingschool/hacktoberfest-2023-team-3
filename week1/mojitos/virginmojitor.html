Virgin Mojito

Ingredients

  • ½ cup loosely packed mint leaves, plus more for the garnish.
  • ½ cup water
  • ½ cup sugar
  • 1 cup lime juice (about 8 limes), plus 1 lime for the garnish
  • 2 cups soda water or club soda
Method
  1. Add the sugar, water and mint to a small saucepan and heat over medium heat.
  2. Bring to a simmer and stir until all sugar is dissolved.
  3. Turn off the heat and allow it to sit for at least 5 minutes.
  4. Then strain it into a large pitcher.
  5. Add the lime juice and rum to the pitcher.
  6. Add the extra mint leaves and the extra lime, cut into rounds.
  7. Fill the pitcher with 4 handfuls of ice.
  8. Gently stir in soda water just before serving.
