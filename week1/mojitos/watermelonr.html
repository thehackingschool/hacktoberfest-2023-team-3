Watermelon Mojito

Ingredients:

  • 6 mint leaves, plus additional for garnish
  • 2 ounce* white rum
  • 1 ounce lime juice
  • 1 ounce simple syrup or maple syrup
  • 2 ounce soda water
  • Ice, for serving (try clear ice)
Method:
  1. Chop the watermelon.
  2. In a cocktail shaker, muddle the mint leaves.
  3. Add the watermelon cubes and muddle again until they are turned into juice.
  4. Shake until cold.
  5. Add ice to a glass, and strain in the drink.
  6. Top off the glass with soda water.
  7. Garnish with additional mint leaves and watermelon wedges.
  8. Muddle 4 cups watermelon into a juice (or use 2 cups watermelon juice).
  9. Add to this Mojito Pitcher recipe, and garnish with additional watermelon slices>
