Biscuit and Jam Shake

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup speculoos cookie spread
  • 1/2 cup whole milk
  • 1 very ripe large banana
  • 8 cups vanilla ice cream
  • 1 cup strawberry preserves
  • 4 whole strawberries, for garnish
Method
  1. In a blender, combine the speculoos, milk, and banana and mix until combined, 1 minute.
  2. Add the ice cream and blend until mixed but still thick, 1 to 2 minutes.
  3. Pour some into pint/parfait glasses alternating with dollops of preserves, creating layers.
  4. Using a long skewer, or a knife, swirl the jam into the shake, have fun with it
  5. Garnish with a strawberry on the edge of the glass and watch the love in your child's eyes shine.
