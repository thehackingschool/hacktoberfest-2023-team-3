Peach Pie Smoothie

Ingredients

  • 2 cups Almond Breeze Almondmilk Origi
  • 2 peaches peeled, pitted and sliced
  • 1 banana
  • 1 cup ice cubes
  • 1 tablespoon honey
  • 1 teaspoon almond extract
Method
  1. Pour the almond milk into the blender.
  2. Add the peaches, banana, ice cubes, honey and almond extract into the blender and blend until smooth.
  3. Garnish with peach slices and serve
